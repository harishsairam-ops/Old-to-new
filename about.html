Our Story

Discover the passion behind Old to Gold

Welcome to Old to Gold

At Old to Gold, we believe that every piece of furniture has a story to tell. Founded in 2015, we specialize in finding, restoring, and curating vintage furniture that combines timeless elegance with modern functionality. Our mission is to bring the beauty of the past into contemporary homes.

Our passion for vintage pieces drives us to search for unique items that have stood the test of time. Each piece in our collection is carefully selected for its quality, character, and potential to transform your living space. We work with skilled craftsmen who restore each item to its former glory while preserving its authentic charm.
